
    Sarbanes-Oxley Act (SOX) - Key Compliance Requirements
    
    Section 302: Corporate Responsibility for Financial Reports
    - CEO and CFO must certify financial reports
    - Officers responsible for establishing and maintaining internal controls
    - Must evaluate effectiveness of internal controls within 90 days
    - Must present conclusions about effectiveness in report
    - Must disclose all significant deficiencies to auditors
    
    Section 404: Management Assessment of Internal Controls
    - Annual report must contain internal control report
    - Management must assess effectiveness of internal controls
    - External auditor must attest to management's assessment
    - Must identify material weaknesses
    - Controls must ensure accurate financial reporting
    
    Section 409: Real Time Issuer Disclosures
    - Companies must disclose material changes on rapid and current basis
    - Information must be in plain English
    - Must include trend and qualitative information
    
    Section 802: Criminal Penalties for Altering Documents
    - Fines and/or up to 20 years imprisonment
    - Applies to destruction, alteration, or falsification of records
    - Auditors must maintain all audit work papers for 5 years
    
    Section 906: Corporate Responsibility for Financial Reports
    - CEO and CFO must certify that financial statements fully comply
    - Knowing violations subject to fines up to $5 million
    - Willful violations up to 20 years imprisonment
    
    Key Control Requirements:
    - Segregation of duties
    - Access controls to systems and data
    - Change management procedures
    - Backup and recovery procedures
    - Audit trails and logging
